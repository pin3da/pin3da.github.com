UTP - 0x7DD To world finals 2014

This utp programming contests team was created in second half of 2013 in order to compete in the 2013 ICPC South america/North regional. The main goal was to achieve a place in the world finals that will take place in Ekaterinburg, Russia.

What is special about this team it's that was a relatively new team with few hours of training together. But after a great endeavor, the team managed to obtain the second place in the south america North regional against teams of 3 other countries besides Colombia: Panama, Venezuela and Ecuador. We are passionate about the problem solving using algorithms and we love to have the possiblity of representing our country in this important competition, not only it's a great honour for us but also we have now the option and the duty to encourage another students to overcome themselves with this kind of challenges.
