UTP - 0x7DD To world finals 2014

the utp programming contests team was created in second half of 2013 in order to compete in the 2013 ICPC South america regional. The main goal was to achieve a place in the world finals that will take place in Ekaterinburg, Russia. What is special about this team it's that was a relatively new team with few hours of training together. But after a great endeavor, the team managed to obtain the second place in the south america North regional against teams of 3 other countries besides colombia, Panama, venezuela and Ecuador. We are passionate about the problem solving using algorithms and we love to have the possiblity of represent our country in this important competition, not only it's a great honour for us but we have now the option and the right to encourage another students to overcome themselves with this kind of challenges.
